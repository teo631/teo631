So here is the techincal part, the game had 5 cars, all modeled by me inspired after real cars, a Lamborghini, a Buggati,a Mustang , a Porche and a Golf. The things that was realy difficult that I got from the internet was the shader, SFXs and music of the pearlecant paint. Also I did add ads if the game blows in TikTok or something (it didn't) so I can advirtise game on other platforms, you can trigger an ad by switching between menus, and crashing also you can watch an ad to skip a level or double the reward cash in a level. The only 2 things I am not happy about the project is the level amount,it's only 25 if you finish all of them game will start you from level 1 saying it's level 26 the other thing is the controls, people i have made test the game often got confused about them expecting car to follow their finger but the actual cotrols are hold right steer right and vice versa.
